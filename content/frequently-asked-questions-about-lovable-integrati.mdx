### What is Aixster's Lovable Integration and how does it work?

Aixster offers a powerful, yet incredibly simple, integration with Lovable, designed to globalize your website effortlessly. The integration allows Aixster to automatically detect and translate your Lovable website content into multiple languages. This process is entirely code-free, meaning you don't need any technical expertise to get started. Once integrated, Aixster monitors your Lovable site for new content and automatically translates it, ensuring your localized versions are always up-to-date.

### Will integrating Aixster with Lovable create duplicate websites?

Absolutely not. A common concern with website translation is the potential for duplicate content issues, which can negatively impact your SEO. Aixster's sophisticated integration with Lovable avoids this entirely. Instead of creating entirely new, duplicate websites, Aixster implements a strategy that utilizes subdomains or subdirectories (e.g., `es.yourdomain.com` or `yourdomain.com/es/`) to serve the translated content. Search engines recognize these as localized versions of your primary site, not duplicates, preserving your SEO rankings and authority. This ensures a clean, SEO-friendly approach to multilingual websites.
